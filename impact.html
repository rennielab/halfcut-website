How we count

Our
method.

Plain language explanation of what each number means, how it's measured, and who signs off on it.

Raised

All funds received into the HalfCut trust account, including donations, campaign revenue, grants, and partner contributions. Independently reconciled quarterly.

Audit note →

To Country

Dollars transferred directly to Traditional Owner partner corporations for on-Country restoration, ranger time, and program delivery. Excludes HalfCut operational costs.

Transfer logs →

Hectares under restoration

Area formally entered into the restoration program, verified each wet season by the Eastern Kuku Yalanji ranger team and cross-checked by an independent ecologist.

Shavers

Registered participants in the August campaign who completed a public shave and raised a verified minimum. Self-reported; we're working on tightening the count for FY26.
