02 Our Position

We are the bridgenot
the saviour.

HalfCut doesn't lead restoration work. We resource the people who already do, the Traditional Owner corporations whose knowledge of this Country predates every map, every border, every treaty.

Our job is to raise the money, hold the governance, publish the audit, and get out of the way. It's quieter than the usual charity story, and it's the only version we think is honest.

Jimmy Langford
Founder · In partnership with Jabalbina Aboriginal Corporation

001 · The Challenge

One of the world's quietest deforestation crises.

Australia is the only developed nation on the global deforestation hotlist. Since 2014, we've cleared more forest than was lost in the entire Amazon during the same period, per capita. Most of it happens on Country that was never ceded.
